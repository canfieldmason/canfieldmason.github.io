Small-Scale Vehicle Body Design & Wind Tunnel Testing

Context: Team-based aerodynamic design project focused on reducing drag for a small-scale vehicle model.

Tools: Fusion 360, SolidWorks Flow Simulation, Wind Tunnel Testing

Engineering Process

  • Modeled a parametric vehicle baseplate for additive manufacturing
  • Developed aerodynamic body iterations and evaluated drag trends
  • Fabricated and tested designs in a wind tunnel

Results

Observed drag trends informed final geometry selection for testing.

Reflection

This project strengthened my understanding of aerodynamic trade studies and the importance of experimental validation.
