Engine Mount Backplate Structural Analysis

Structural finite element pre-processing of a small two-stroke engine mounting backplate, including geometry reduction, material assignment, localized mesh refinement at bolt holes and bearing interfaces, and application of representative static loads and constraints.

Note: Model setup is complete; solution execution was limited by ANSYS Student license element restrictions.

Formula SAE Chassis Structural Analysis

Structural analysis of a Formula SAE tubular spaceframe chassis completed through ANSYS Innovation Space. The project focused on line-body modeling, tube property assignment, and evaluation under torsional stiffness, cornering, combined aerodynamic loading, and frontal impact load cases.

Baja SAE Roll Cage Structural Analysis

Finite element analysis of a Baja SAE roll cage structure considering front, side, rear, rollover, and torsional loading scenarios. Emphasis was placed on structural load paths, driver safety, and interpretation of deformation and stress results under impact-like conditions.
